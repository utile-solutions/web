About

Software engineer specializing in React and TypeScript

Focused on improving user experience in modern web applications.

I work with SaaS and web app teams to find and fix the small UX issues that quietly frustrate users — misalignments, inconsistent components, confusing flows, and subtle friction that accumulates into a poor first impression.

The issues I find are rarely dramatic. They're 10px alignment problems. Hover states that don't match. Form errors that don't explain themselves. Each one is small. Together, they define whether your product feels finished or not.

I don't produce reports that sit in a Notion doc. I produce specific, actionable fixes — the kind your team can implement this sprint.
